Process

From files to finished model — engineered for accuracy and speed.

Each step is optimized to support tight development schedules and presentation-ready results. We combine advanced digital manufacturing with traditional craftsmanship.

01/ 05

Design Review & Planning

Before any fabrication begins, we review all provided drawings, plans, and 3D models. We confirm scale, level of detail, materials, finishes, special requests, and delivery expectations — and identify how the model will be used.

  • Scope alignment
  • Scale selection
  • Audience & goal
02/ 05

Model Engineering

Once scope is confirmed, we prepare the digital model for fabrication. We clean, simplify, or rebuild your geometry to ensure accuracy at the selected scale — ensuring the finished model is clean, accurate, and manufacturable.

  • Geometry cleanup
  • Scale optimization
  • Manufacturability
03/ 05

Fabrication

We combine advanced digital manufacturing — laser cutting, CNC, and 3D printing — with hand craftsmanship. Models are built from durable materials suitable for transport and presentation.

  • Laser & CNC
  • 3D printing
  • Hand assembly
04/ 05

Detailing, Paint & QC

Finishing, painting, and assembly bring the model to its final standard. Quality control photos are shared throughout so you always know where things stand.

  • Finishing
  • Lighting
  • Quality control
05/ 05

Delivery & Setup

Your model is professionally packed and arrives presentation-ready — easy to unpack and install. For local projects, we offer on-site installation.

  • Professional packing
  • Nationwide shipping
  • On-site install
