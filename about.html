About

We carve every model with purpose.

Carve Model is a Los Angeles-based model-making studio crafting architectural models, sales-gallery pieces, product prototypes, film miniatures, and art installations — built for moments where clarity matters: approvals, presentations, filming, and internal decision-making. We work with architects, developers, general contractors, agencies, production teams, product designers, and artists across the U.S. and Canada.

Our approach

Built for alignment, not decoration.

Our clients include architects, developers, production designers, agencies, and product teams across the United States and Canada who rely on physical models not as decoration, but as tools for alignment and action.

Digital tools are powerful — but important decisions are still made around tables, in rooms, and in real space. We focus on what a model needs to clarify, not what it needs to impress. Every model is tailored to its purpose — nothing more, nothing less.
