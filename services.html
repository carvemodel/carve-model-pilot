Services

Full-service model making, managed in-house.

We provide custom physical model making supporting architectural design, product development, and film previsualization — a turnkey process covering scope, engineering, fabrication, finishing, and logistics.

01 — Architecture

Architectural Scale Models

From simplified block models for early design to detailed replicas highlighting façade, materials, glazing, and structure. Built for planning approvals, stakeholder presentations, investor meetings, and sales galleries.

  • Massing & concept models for entitlement and planning reviews
  • Detailed presentation models for clients and public exhibitions
  • Large-scale models for multi-building developments — massing, circulation, site
02 — Marketing

Sales Gallery & Presentation Models

High-finish display models designed to make a strong visual impression. Detailed finishes, lighting options, and premium presentation bases for marketing suites and investment pitches.

  • Lit models with integrated LED and acrylic detailing
  • Premium bases, vitrines, and branded plinths
  • Built to anchor sales centers and launch environments
03 — Product

Product Prototypes

We support product designers and engineers by transforming sketches and CAD files into fully built physical mockups — for design validation, demos, and investor conversations.

  • CAD-to-physical mockups for hardware startups
  • Appearance and form-study models
  • Finish, material, and scale exploration
04 — Film

Film & Commercial Miniatures

We work closely with art departments to design and fabricate miniature sets for previsualization and cinematic planning. Crafted from lightweight, film-grade materials, ready for transport or on-location staging.

  • Previsualization sets for cinematic planning
  • Commercial & product miniatures
  • Lightweight, location-ready builds
05 — Site

Topographic & Site Models

Accurate representations of terrain, hardscape, and landscape — roads, water features, and grade changes. Ideal for planning, design reviews, and wayfinding studies.

  • Contour terrain and grade modeling
  • Landscape, roads, and water features
  • Context massing for surrounding sites
06 — Art

Art Installations & Sculpture

We collaborate with artists, designers, and cultural institutions to fabricate sculptural works and installation pieces — translating a concept or maquette into a precise, durable, exhibition-ready object.

  • Maquette-to-final fabrication and scaling-up
  • Mixed-material builds for gallery & public display
  • Mounting, finishing, and install-ready delivery

The right first step is understanding what decision your model needs to support. Tell us about your project and we'll recommend the scope.
